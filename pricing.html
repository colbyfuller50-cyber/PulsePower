Pricing & Billing

$5.00 / hour

Only pay for the time you keep the battery checked out. Time is prorated to the minute.

$45 authorization hold

An authorization hold is placed at checkout in case the battery isn’t returned. Once you return the charger, the hold is released and you’re charged only for the rental time.

Examples

Quick top‑up (45 min)

45 minutes × $5/hr ≈ $3.75.

Out for lunch (2 hours)

2 hours × $5/hr = $10.

Busy afternoon (6 hours)

6 hours × $5/hr = $30.

Policies

  • Returns: Return to any Pulse Power station to stop the timer.
  • Not returned: If a battery isn’t returned within 48 hours, the $45 hold may convert to a replacement charge.
  • Damage: If a cable or unit is damaged, return it and notify support so we can swap it out.
  • Receipts: An itemized receipt is emailed after return.
