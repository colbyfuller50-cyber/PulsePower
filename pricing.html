Pricing & Billing

$5.00 / hour

Only pay for the time you keep the battery checked out.

$45 hold

Authorization hold placed at checkout in case the battery isn’t returned. When you return the charger, the hold is automatically released and you’re only charged the rental time.

Examples

Quick top‑up (45 min)

45 minutes × $5/hr ≈ $3.75.

Out for lunch (2 hours)

2 hours × $5/hr = $10.

Busy afternoon (6 hours)

6 hours × $5/hr = $30.

Policies

  • Returns: Return to any PulsePower station to stop the timer.
  • Lost / not returned: If a battery isn’t returned within 48 hours, the $45 hold may convert to a replacement charge.
  • Damage: If a cable or unit is damaged, return it and notify support so we can swap it out.
  • Receipts: An itemized receipt is emailed after return.
