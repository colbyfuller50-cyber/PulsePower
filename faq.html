Frequently Asked Questions

1) How much does it cost?

$5.00 per hour with a daily cap of $45.

2) How do I return?

Insert the battery into any PulsePower station. Billing stops immediately.

3) What if I don’t return it?

If not returned within 48 hours, a replacement fee may apply.

4) Which phones are supported?

iPhone (Lightning) and most Android (USB‑C). Many phones also support wireless charging with your own cable.

5) Do I need my own cable?

No—our batteries include Lightning and USB‑C cables.

6) Can I keep charging while walking?

Yes. Batteries are portable so you can move freely.

7) How long does a battery last?

Usually 1–2 full phone charges depending on device and usage.

8) Can I rent more than one?

Yes, if the station has availability.

9) Is there a deposit?

No deposit. You pay only for time used.

10) Are batteries sanitized?

Yes, we routinely inspect, sanitize, and recharge all returns.

11) What if a cable is damaged?

Return it and grab another battery; then email support so we can service it.

12) How do I see nearby stations?

Check the Locations page; a live map is coming soon.

13) Can I reserve ahead?

Not yet; rentals are first‑come, first‑serve.

14) Do you support events?

Yes! Email us to arrange bulk rentals and temporary stations.

15) What’s your support email?

pulsepowerrental@gmail.com
