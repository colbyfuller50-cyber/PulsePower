Frequently Asked Questions

1) How much does it cost?

$5.00 per hour. A $45 hold is authorized at checkout and released when you return the battery; you only pay for your rental time.

2) Where can I return?

To any PulsePower station. Returning to a different station is okay as long as it’s a PulsePower unit.

3) What if I forget to return?

We send reminders. After 48 hours, the $45 hold may convert to a replacement charge.

4) Do I need my own cable?

No. Each battery includes Lightning and USB‑C cables built in.

5) Is wireless charging supported?

Our packs are cable‑based for speed and compatibility. You can use your own wireless puck if desired.

6) How many charges will I get?

Typically 1–2 full phone charges depending on device and usage.

7) Can I rent more than one battery?

Yes, if the station has stock.

8) What devices are compatible?

Most modern phones and accessories using Lightning or USB‑C connectors.

9) Are the units safe?

Yes. Our batteries are certified and undergo routine inspection and sanitation.

10) Can I walk around while charging?

Absolutely. The battery is portable and designed for on‑the‑go use.

11) Do you charge partial hours?

Time is prorated to the minute. You pay only for time used.

12) What happens if a cable is broken?

Return the unit and grab another. Email support so we can service the damaged one.

13) Do you take cash?

No. We accept major cards and digital wallets via kiosk checkout.

14) Can I reserve ahead?

Not yet — first‑come, first‑serve. A live availability map is planned.

15) Who do I contact?

Email pulsepowerrental@gmail.com and we’ll help.

⚡ Reminder: please return once fully charged so someone else can power up.
