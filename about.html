About Pulse Power

Pulse Power is a Columbia‑based battery rental station service that keeps your devices alive while you’re on the go. Our mission is to make staying charged simple, fair, and available wherever you need it.

What makes us different

  • Convenience: grab‑and‑go rentals with built‑in Lightning & USB‑C.
  • Transparent pricing: $5/hr with a refundable $45 hold.
  • Flexible returns: return to any Pulse Power station.
  • Reliable hardware: routinely cleaned, inspected, and recharged.
